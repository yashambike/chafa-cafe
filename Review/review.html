Chafa coffee was recommended to me by many friends, and as a coffee/tea lover I was very excited to check them out. I've also heard their bagel sandwiches are delicious. The interior of the coffee shop is very modern, with a few tables/seats and very unique bleacher style seating farther in the back. There were a few people studying/doing work but the ambiance seems much better for chit chat. The customer service was great, my friend and I were able to chat with one of the workers and we learned more about the shop and how they source their beans/matcha. My friend got a cafe latte which she liked and I got the seasonal caramel pumpkin spice latte. Although pricy (I think my cup was $7ish), the quality and flavor was delicious. Not to sweet, deep coffee flavor, and perfect balance of caramel and pumpkin spice. We sat at the bar and saw the kitchen churn out bagel sandwich after bagel sandwich, all made fresh to order and looked so delicious. I definitely will come back to try more of their menu (most notably their matcha latte) and their bagels! My favorite coffee shop in Boston so far..

martin d
